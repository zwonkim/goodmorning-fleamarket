# Brand Guideline

## Brand Keywords

Warm Morning Neighborhood Friendly Playful

## Mascot

The provided SVG mascot assets are the official Good Morning mascot.

These assets are the single source of truth.

Do NOT redraw, recreate or reinterpret the mascot.

Always use the provided SVG assets whenever possible.

Recommended usage:

-   Login
-   Welcome Banner
-   Empty State
-   Loading State
-   Error State
-   Upload Success
-   Profile Header

The mascot should support the experience rather than dominate the
interface.

## Illustration Style

-   Flat colors
-   Bold outline
-   Rounded shapes
-   Minimal shadows

## Tone of Voice

Friendly Optimistic Encouraging
